## Recommended Research Workflow

### Phase 1: Digital Collection (Current)
1. ✅ Library of Congress images downloaded
2. ⬜ DC History Center - request high-res images
3. ⬜ Search Chronicling America for newspaper articles
4. ⬜ Check Internet Archive for digitized Tenleytown books

### Phase 2: Deep Dive Topics
1. **Civil War Era**
   - Fort Reno/Fort Pennsylvania military records
   - Maps of DC defenses
   - Battle of Fort Stevens accounts

2. **Reno City (African American History)**
   - Census records 1870-1920
   - Jesse Reno School records
   - Condemnation/displacement documents

3. **Streetcar Era**
   - Tennallytown and Rockville Railway records
   - Route maps and schedules
   - Photographs of cars and infrastructure

4. **Broadcasting History**
   - WRC-TV archives
   - Jim Henson/Sam and Friends materials
   - Nixon-Kennedy debate footage/photos

### Phase 3: In-Person Research
- Schedule appointment at DC History Center
- Visit Tenley-Friendship Library local history collection
- Contact Tenleytown Historical Society
- Request specific items from LOC reading rooms
