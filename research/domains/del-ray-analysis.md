## Recommendation Notes

### Top Choice: delrayhistory.com
This is the clear winner for a neighborhood history site:
- **16 characters** - short and memorable
- **"history"** - immediately communicates the site's purpose
- **.com TLD** - most recognizable and trusted
- **Professional** - sounds like an authoritative resource

### Strong Alternatives
- **delrayalmanac.com** - Best if you want to emphasize the "almanac" brand consistency across multiple neighborhood sites
- **delraychronicle.com** - Best if aiming for a journalistic, narrative-driven approach
- **delrayheritage.com** - Best if focusing on preservation and cultural significance

### Geographic Considerations
- Del Ray is in Alexandria, VA, but "delray" alone is sufficient for local recognition
- Adding "va" (e.g., delrayvahistory.com) provides geographic disambiguation from Del Ray Beach, FL
- Adding "alexandria" makes domains too long (e.g., delrayalexandriahistory.com = 26 chars)

### Domains to Avoid
- Avoid "wiki" suffix (implies user-edited content, less authoritative)
- Avoid "buzz/beat/scene" (too casual for history site)
- Avoid .info/.co/.net TLDs (less trusted than .com/.org)
