# Neighborhood History Sites Business Plan

## Executive Summary
Network of 16 hyperlocal history websites covering DC/MD/VA neighborhoods. Flask-based with AI-assisted content generation. Multi-tenant architecture for cost efficiency.

## The Network (16 Sites)

**DC (10)**: Tenleytown, Brightwood, Kalorama, Columbia Heights, H Street, SW Waterfront, Anacostia, Shepherd Park, Glover Park, Woodley Park

**MD (3)**: College Park, Hyattsville, Potomac

**VA (3)**: Falls Church, Vienna, Del Ray

## Revenue Model
- **Primary**: Local business sponsorships ($100-500/sponsor)
- **Secondary**: Display ads (AdSense)
- **Tertiary**: Affiliate (local tours, books)

## Financial Projections

### Low Scenario (Flop - 40% probability)
- Traffic: <1K visits/month total
- Revenue: $0
- Net: **-$206/year**
- Outcome: Hobby project, portfolio piece

### Medium Scenario (Break-even - 35% probability)
- Traffic: 5-10K visits/month
- Sponsors: 1-2 @ $100-200/each
- Revenue: $200-400/year
- Net: **~$0/year**
- Outcome: Self-sustaining hobby

### High Scenario (Modest Success - 20% probability)
- Traffic: 20K+ visits/month
- Sponsors: 4-6 local businesses
- Ad revenue: $50-100/month
- Net: **+$1,200-2,400/year**
- Outcome: Nice side income

### Best Case (5% probability)
- Traffic: 50K+ visits/month
- Multiple sponsors per neighborhood
- Net: **+$5,000-12,000/year**

## Key Metrics to Track
- Traffic per neighborhood site
- Sponsor conversion rate
- Email list growth
- Community event attendance
- Search rankings for "[neighborhood] history"

## Strategic Priorities
1. Deploy top 5 highest-potential sites first (Tenleytown, Kalorama, Potomac, Vienna, Del Ray)
2. Focus sponsor outreach on affluent neighborhoods
3. Partner with neighborhood associations
4. Cross-promote with Tenleytown Main Street
5. Add event aggregation for community value

## Content Strategy
- Historical research from Library of Congress, DC History Center
- AI-generated atmospheric images (clearly labeled)
- Automated event scraping
- 2,000-4,000 words per neighborhood

## Risk Factors
- Low traffic in competitive local space
- Sponsor sales requires active outreach
- Content accuracy concerns
- Maintenance burden across 16 sites

## 12-Month Target
- 5 sites fully deployed
- 10K monthly visitors across network
- 2-3 paying sponsors
- Break-even on operating costs
