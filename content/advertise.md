## Why Advertise Here?

### We're Different

Most local directories just list your address and hours. We tell your story:
- When did your business open?
- Who founded it and why?
- What was in your space before?
- How does it connect to neighborhood history?

This context makes your listing memorable and builds genuine community connection.

### Targeted Local Audience

Our readers are:
- Residents of Tenleytown, AU Park, Spring Valley, Van Ness, and surrounding areas
- People researching the neighborhood (prospective residents, local history enthusiasts)
- American University students, faculty, and staff
- Local workers seeking nearby businesses

### Community Trust

We're not a faceless review platform. We're a locally-focused site with deep roots in neighborhood history. Being featured here is an endorsement of your place in the community fabric.

## What We Research

For Featured Listings and Sponsored Content, we dig into:

- **Property history** - What businesses occupied your space going back decades?
- **Building history** - When was your building constructed? By whom?
- **Street context** - How has your block evolved?
- **Neighborhood connections** - How does your business fit the area's story?

We use historical newspapers, city records, and community archives to uncover stories you might not know about your own location.

## FAQ

**How is this different from Yelp or Google?**
We focus on story, not reviews. Your listing includes historical context that builds real connection with the community.

**Can I write my own history section?**
Absolutely. We can also research it for you, or combine your knowledge with our research.

**How long do Featured listings run?**
Monthly, with discounts for 6+ month commitments.

**Do you accept all businesses?**
We focus on businesses serving Upper Northwest DC. We reserve the right to decline listings that don't fit our community focus.

**Can I update my listing?**
Yes. Featured listings include monthly updates. Basic listings can be updated anytime via email.
